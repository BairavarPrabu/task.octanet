Hackathon Information

Hackathon Name: Innovative Hackathon 2024
Date: February 6-7 , 2024
Venue: Rajamani Auditorium, Psna College
Description: Join us for a thrilling 24-hour coding marathon! Showcase your skills, collaborate with fellow students, and build innovative solutions to real-world problems.
Instructions:

  • Form a team of 3-5 members .
  • Each team will be provided with access to the internet, power supplies, and refreshments.
  • Participants are required to bring their own laptops and other necessary equipment.
  • All submissions should be made before the deadline.
  • The hackathon will be evaluated based on creativity, technical difficulty, and real-world applicability.
